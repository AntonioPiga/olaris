Section:
  nuv devel ferretdb 

Usage:
  ferretdb find <collection>  
  ferretdb submit <jsonfile> 

Commands:
  find    find for documents into the given ferretbd/Mongodb collection
  submit  use  {insert: <collection>,documents: [ <document>, <document>, <document>, ... ]} to insert documents into a collection. (@see https://www.mongodb.com/docs/manual/reference/command/), for a full reference.
